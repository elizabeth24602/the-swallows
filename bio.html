Our Story

Discovering the music of The Swallows, is a lot like finding yourself at a party with trusted friends … the kind who know all your secrets, share great memories and funny stories. The Swallows are a musical collaboration of four girls Beki, Sienna, Lola and Rachel. The girl’s met at an open mic night during their first year of university. They recognized an instant chemistry and a shared perspective; an appreciation for life’s moments, ironies and quirky little truths. The band began to write together, while also growing more in and Manchester. The Swallows quickly bloomed as regulars at Music City honkytonks, and as house concert favorites. Soon, they began playing concert venues and festivals across the UK. They write and perform relatable songs about real life and real challenges, about people they’ve met and experiences they’ve endured, all with a refreshing sense of humor, optimism and occasional, satisfying sarcasm. While cleverly turning a phrase and weaving a tale, their appeal is punctuated by musicianship and skilled harmonies. Onstage and off, they engage their fans as friends, and maintain those relationships long after the equipment is loaded into the van. Their fans are fervent, following them to shows, and providing support with travel, lodging, recording costs and more. A new page in The Swallows' story, one that continues to unfold with inspiration, creativity, and some great big dreams.

“Evoking the timeless sorcery of four-part harmonies is one thing. Penning songs that put this gift to good use is another.
The Swallows possess both."
